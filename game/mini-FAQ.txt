--------------------------- Mini-FAQ ----------------------------------------------
Q: How can I use my saves?
A: Click start, then right-click anywhere inside the game to open a menu where you can use the saves.

Q: How do I change the text appearance rate?
A: Click start, then right-click anywhere inside the game to open a menu. Next, go to the preferences section, the settings are there.

Q: I want to play in another language, what should I do?
A: Unfortunately, only English is supported.

Q: The music is too loud, how do I change the volume?
A: Click start, then right-click anywhere inside the game to open a menu. Next, go to the preferences section, the settings are there.
